# Radhika Nagpal and Swarm Robotics

This repository contains an academic seminar paper written as part of a university proseminar on pioneers in artificial intelligence.

The paper focuses on Radhika Nagpal’s contributions to swarm robotics, collective behavior, self-organizing systems, and bio-inspired artificial intelligence. It discusses how decentralized systems can solve complex problems through cooperation, robustness, and simple local interactions.

## Topics

- Artificial Intelligence
- Swarm Robotics
- Collective Behavior
- Bio-inspired Systems
- Decentralized Intelligence
- Radhika Nagpal’s Research Contributions

## Context

This paper was written as part of a university proseminar at TU Dortmund University.
